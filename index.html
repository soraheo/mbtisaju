🇰🇷 New to Saju? Read the 30-second intro⌄

Saju (사주) — the "Four Pillars of Destiny" — is traditional Korean astrology. It maps the energy of the year, month, day and hour you were born into four "pillars," each written with characters tied to the five elements. The balance between them describes your natural temperament, relationships, money flow and life rhythm. Koreans still check it before marriages, business launches and big decisions.

Like a birth chartIt's the Korean cousin of your zodiac sign or birth chart — read from your exact birth date and time, not just your month.
Why time mattersYour birth hour sets one of the four pillars. Don't know it? You'll still get a full year/month/day reading.
Five elementsWood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water. Which ones run strong or missing in you shapes your strengths and stress style.

Why MBTI + Saju? MBTI shows how your mind works today; Saju shows the energy you were born with. Together they give a fuller, more personal picture — and a mental-care plan that actually fits you.
